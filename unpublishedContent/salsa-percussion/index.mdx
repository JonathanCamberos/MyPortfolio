# Pattern: Salsa Percussion, Poncho Sanchez
10/04/25

Audio: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0j23EFFzFF8

## Mambo

### Congas: Supertumba, Tumba, Conga, Timestamp 6:09

Supertumba: E
Tumba: G
Conga: C

Holding the conga:
- tilted towards the tumba
- held in between feet and thighs

Left Hand Sounds:
- (S) Slap: Hit Edge with Fingers and don't lift off, RH mutes
- (O) Open Tone: Hit edge with palm of hand and lift off, RH no mute.

Right Hand Sounds:
- (T) Tip: With hand tilted after heel or resting on drum, hit with fingers
- (H) Heel: With hand tilted after tip or resting on drum, hit with heel

Mambo Pattern: Basic Three Point Shuffle

Intro:
- Add an 'open' pickup on the '1' of the intro bar. The rest of the 
slaps and opens will start on the '+'.

```python 

        | Intro                                 | Repeat                                |
    LH: | O   S   |     O O |     S   |     O O |     S   |     O O |     S   |     O O |
    RH: |   T   T | H T     | H T   T | H T     | H T   T | H T     | H T   T | H T     |
        | 1 e + a | 2 e + a | 3 e + a | 4 e + a |   e   a |   e   a |   e   a |   e   a |

```

With a 2-3 clave it sounds like:
```python

        | Intro                                 | Repeat                                |
    23: |     *   | *       | *     * |     *   |     *   | *       | *     * |     *   |
    LH: | O   S   |     O O |     S   |     O O |     S   |     O O |     S   |     O O |
    RH: |   T   T | H T     | H T   T | H T     | H T   T | H T     | H T   T | H T     |
```

Mambo Pattern: Three Point Shuffle w/ Tumba

Now lets add the tumbas to the 3 point shuffle, which now 
makes it a 2 bar phrase when you add the tumba 'dora'.

Note: Tumbas are always played on the 3 side of the clave, no
mater if the clave is 2-3 or 3-2.

```python 

        | Intro                                 | Repeat                                |
    23: |     *   | *       | *     * |     *   |     *   | *       | *     * |     *   |
    LH: | O   S   |     O O |     S   |     O O |     S   |     O O |     S   |     O O |
    TB: |         |         |       O | O       |         |         |       O | O       |
    RH: |   T   T | H T     | H T     |   T     | H T   T | H T     | H T     |   T     |
        |   e   a |   e   a |   e   a |   e   a |   e   a |   e   a |   e   a |   e   a |

```

### Bongos: Timestamp 10:57

Bongos: Tuned Higher than conga

Holding the bongos:
- Tilted in between legs
- Higher drum towards dominant hand

Left Hand Sounds:
- (T) Tips: Hit with tips of 4 fingers
- (S) Thumb Side: Hit with meaty part below thumb side of palm

Right Hand sounds:
- (H) Tips: hit high bongo with tips of 4 fingers
- (L) Tips: hit low bongo with tips of 4 fingers
- (A) Accent: With tips of 4 fingers, hit the edge of drum for accent, using during improvisation

Intro: 
- Add a 'low' pickup on the '1' of the intro bar. The rest of the 
slaps and opens will start on the '+'. This overlaps with other pickups on
other instruments such as the conga pickups.
